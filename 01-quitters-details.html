Cancer gave up on me twice

  • Home
  • In times of test, family is best.

" HELLO, Hey! young man! Stop consuming tobacco ! Look at the damage it's done to my tongue!"
Exclaims Mr. Sachin Manohar Handage , when he observes today's youth chewing tobacco. He has an approach of “Maybe! I can stop the younger generation, who, like me, started a tobacco addiction in their early twenties. “ So , as we say ! “Everything happens for a reason !” One affected , can inspire the youth !

       In 2015 , Mr Sachin Manohar Handage got a mouth ulcer and a venous clot near his throat. He neglected it since he assumed it was normal, but it did not heal for another two months. When he visited the doctor , the doctor said there is a chance of Mouth Cancer . Ofcourse! It shook him off ! In his mind, a slew of questions arose... Is it possible to cure this cancer? Is everything going to be okay? How much will it cost at least? He was terrified. At such moments, everyone needs someone to offer them strength and reassure them that everything will be fine. His little brother, being a pharmacist, did an extensive research on cancer treatments and cures, as well as a hunt for cancer treatment health centers where he might find good doctors to treat Mr. Sachin .

       Mr Sachin gave up tobacco as soon as he was cognizant that it was causing him cancer! After 6 months of radiations and chemotherapies , he eventually gained faith in himself and realized that he had overcome cancer! . At this point of time, he was reborn. He had maintained his composure during the entire process and spent roughly 6-7 lakhs for the cancer treatment . He gave up all of his bad habits and addictions, including smoking, drinking, and even eating non-vegetarian food .

        Unfortunately, his mouth cancer recurred in 2018 i.e after 3 years of the treatment , owing to anxiety , mental stress and daily slog .This time he was under a lot of pressure . It was shocking that despite taking so many measures and receiving treatment at Tata Hospital, he was still diagnosed with cancer . He was already frustrated when this happened and then he met someone in the hospital who gave him some ayurvedic recommendations, which made his life much easier. Keeping a positive outlook on the situation which is getting repeated unfortunately, he subsequently began using ayurvedic and homeopathic therapies, which he found effective for his body .

       Mr. Sachin, a courageous man, claims that ayurveda had such a profound influence on him that he didn't even require chemotherapy or radiation treatments the second time around. The total cost was around 2 to 2.5 lakhs in the 2nd treatment . Unfortunately, he went through a tongue incision during this operation .

       He believes that exercising consistently, eating greens, and avoiding habits such as smoking, consuming alcoholic beverages are all ways to live a healthy lifestyle. Mr. Handage is indeed a true inspiration to all cancer survivors who are fighting this life-threatening disease. Mr. Sachin is a very sporty person who is always willing to help others in his community who are dealing with cancer. Mr. Sachin's advice to today's youth is to steer away from any negative addictions, which generally begin in their early twenties.He also gave a big advice to choose our friend circle wisely , as –“ As much as people refuse to believe it , but the company you keep does have an impact and influence on your choices . “
